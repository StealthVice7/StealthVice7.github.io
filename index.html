Shrek Prayer Room

Our Ogre,

Who art in His swamp,

hallowed be Thy onion;

Thy ogres come;

Thy will be done on earth as it is in His swamp;

Give us this day our daily onion:

and forgive us our trespasses on others' swamps

as we forgive those who trespass against our swamps,

and lead us not into temptation,

but deliver us from Farquaad.

Shrek is love,

Shrek is life.
